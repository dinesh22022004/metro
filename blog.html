Top Picks for monsoon

Discover the must-have shoes for your monsoon wardrobe.

Comfort and Style

Explore shoes that combine comfort and style for all-day wear.

  • Rubber or Waterproof Shoes: Shoes made of rubber or other waterproof materials are ideal for the monsoon as they can keep your feet dry even in heavy rain. Rain boots or rubber sandals are good options.
  • Sandals with Straps: Sandals with secure straps are a good choice as they provide ventilation and are easy to dry in case they get wet. Look for sandals with good grip to prevent slipping on wet surfaces.
  • Quick-drying Shoes: Shoes made of materials that dry quickly, such as mesh or synthetic fabrics, can be convenient during the monsoon as they won't stay wet for long.
